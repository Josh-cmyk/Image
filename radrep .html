We provide information about the pricing of repairs online in advance so you know in advance how much a repair will cost you.

FAST

The average waiting time at a static bike shop is 3 days. Ordering with FIXI takes 5 minutes. Repair will take less than 45 minutes. Get back on the road fast.

FAIR

Improving lives is a priority for us. We provide a professional and friendly mobile bicycle repairs for a similar price to static bike shops (excluding travel cost). 


CONVENIENT

No need to stress about finding out if there is a local bike shop that will be open at times that fit with your work schedule.

Please use the form to place your order. Include in the message:       

  1.  Your phone number
  2.  Full address (and name on bell) of the desired location
  3. Appointment time(s)/your availability
  4. Type of repairs required. 
  5. If the bike has a flat tire, please specify the dimensions (these can be found on the tire, or wheel and will be in the following formats: 37-622 or 700x28C), and whether you need a new tire or an inner tube.
